How Afya-Chain Works

Three simple steps to secure, interoperable health data

1. Register with NIDA

Patients register using their National ID (NIDA) as a unique identifier. Your identity is securely verified.

2. Health Records on Blockchain

Medical records from hospitals and clinics are stored immutably on a decentralized ledger. You own your data.

3. Consent-Based Access

You control who can access your records. Doctors request access, and you approve or revoke with one click.
